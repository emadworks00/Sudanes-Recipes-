sudanese porridge

the sudanes porridge is a popular sudanese cuisine,
and has its own vlue on social events and concerts

    ingredients

  • 150 g flour
  • 500 ml 2 cup of water
  • 200 ml a cup of yogurt
  • 5 g 1 teaspoon salt

    steps

  1. mix a cup of water, a cup of flour and a cup of yogurt. stir the mixture well until combined

  2. in another pot, put a cup of water on high heat, leave it until it boils, then add salt and stir

  3. add the mixture to the water, and stir well, quickly and continuously, until the porridge is cooked and holds togrther.

  4. place it in a deep bowl greased with oil until it cools. transfer it to a serving plate and serve it with the mallah weika.
