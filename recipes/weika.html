sudanese mallah weika

the sudanes mallah is a popular sudanese cuisine, and has its own vlue on social events and concerts

    ingredients

  • 2 large onion
  • 1/4 kiloMinced meet
  • 3 pills tomatoes medium
  • 2 tablespoon sauce
  • 1/3 cup of tea quantity of oil
  • 3 tablespoon wike
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • m pcoriander
  • m ppepper
  • shamar

    steps

  1. cut the onion in the oil and let it turn yellow.

  2. add chopped tomatoes and give them a space.

  3. in a second solution, i add the minced meet in a little bit of oil, and i add a little water to it . after it is cooked, i add the sauce to it , leave it for while, and drian the oil from it into a cup.

  4. add the meet to previous mixture and mix them with a blender.

  5. put them back in the pot and add the spices, garlic, salt, and yogurt. leave them for a while and add the oil before adding the meet and sauce, and viola.
