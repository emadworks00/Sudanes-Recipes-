sudanese ghariba

the sudanes ghariba is a popular sudanese cuisine, and has its own vlue on social events and concerts

    ingredients

  • 1 cup soft sugar
  • 1 cup ghee
  • 2 and half cup of sprecise
  • sprinkle bakkenpowder
  • little salt
  • to decorate: cinnamon

    steps

  1. put sugar and ghee in the blender or electric mixer and beat them well for a minute

  2. then put the mixture in a large bowl and add a sprinkle of salt and baking powder

  3. then add the flour gradually until it blends together

  4. we put the dough in the refrigertor for half an hour, then we shape it like a ball and put cinnamon in half.

  5. place in the oven for 20 minuts (do not brown too much or crack)
